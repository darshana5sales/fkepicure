About FK Epicure Foods

A frozen-food plant
with a restaurant's palate.

FK Epicure Foods LLP manufactures authentic Indian frozen food at its plant in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. Every recipe is rooted in professional kitchen experience — cooked in controlled batches, then individually quick frozen (IQF) within hours to lock in texture, nutrition and flavour without preservatives.

Our range spans 120+ SKUs across 11 categories — from frozen appetizers and base gravies to ready-to-eat meals, Indian breads, sweets and vegetables — supplied ready-to-cook and ready-to-eat to commercial kitchens and retail freezers worldwide.

Our products reach the market under our flagship brand IndiFrozen — and as private label for partners across the globe.

Manufacturing

Built to freeze food the honest way.

Our plant in Ahmedabad pairs restaurant craft with industrial discipline — handcrafted where it matters, precision freezing where it counts. Dedicated lines for appetizers, gravies, breads and sweets run alongside blast freezers and IQF tunnels, with temperature-mapped cold storage and export documentation under one roof.

IQF Freezing Lines

Each piece frozen individually within hours of cooking — loose, restaurant-quality pieces with no clumping.

Blast Freezing & Cold Storage

Rapid core-temperature pull-down to -18°C and temperature-mapped storage until dispatch.

Hand-Finish Sections

Shaping, stuffing and garnish stages kept by hand for authentic form and texture.

Export-Ready Packing

Retail pouches, food-service cartons and custom private-label formats with compliant labelling.
