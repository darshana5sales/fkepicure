Our Story

Born in a kitchen
of friends.

It began the way the best Indian meals do — with friends around one table. What started as Friends Kitchen, a single food-service venture in Ahmedabad, grew into a family of brands united by one stubborn belief: food made with patience tastes different.

Today, FK Epicure Foods is the house that holds those tables together. We run restaurants and cafés where recipes are still tempered by hand, and a frozen-foods operation that carries the same recipes — individually quick frozen at peak freshness — to commercial kitchens across 35+ countries.

Different formats, one kitchen philosophy: source honestly, cook slowly, serve generously.

Our Philosophy

Three rules of the house.

✦

Recipes Before Trends

Every dish across our brands begins with a recipe perfected on a real restaurant table — not in a boardroom. Trends fade; a great dal fry doesn't.

❋

Honest Sourcing

Farm-fresh produce, single-origin spices and trusted local suppliers. We know where every ingredient comes from, because our own name is on every plate.

✺

Slow Where It Matters

Gravies simmered for hours, breads rested properly, sweets reduced the old way. Speed belongs in our logistics — never in our cooking.

Manufacturing & Infrastructure

A kitchen built to export scale.

Our production facility on the Sarkhej–Sanand Highway, Ahmedabad, is where restaurant craft meets industrial discipline — handcrafted processes where they matter, precision technology where it counts.

Dedicated lines for snacks, gravies, breads and sweets run alongside blast freezers and IQF tunnels, with cold storage and export documentation handled under one roof.

IQF Freezing Lines

Individually quick frozen for loose, restaurant-quality pieces — no clumping, no sogginess.

Blast Freezing & Cold Storage

Rapid core-temperature pull-down and temperature-mapped storage until dispatch.

Hand-Finish Sections

Shaping, stuffing and garnish stages kept by hand — because machines can't fold a samosa with love.

Export-Ready Packing

Retail pouches, food-service cartons and custom private-label formats, FDA-compliant labelling.
