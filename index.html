About Us

A frozen-food plant
with a restaurant's palate.

FK Epicure Foods LLP manufactures authentic Indian frozen food at its plant in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. The company grew out of Friends Kitchen — our restaurant and catering brand — so every recipe on our lines was proven at a real table long before it was scaled to a production run.

Cooked in controlled batches and individually quick frozen (IQF) within hours, our products hold their texture, nutrition and flavour without preservatives. The range spans 120+ SKUs across 11 categories — appetizers, base gravies, ready-to-eat meals, Indian breads, sweets and vegetables — supplied ready-to-cook and ready-to-eat to commercial kitchens and retail freezers worldwide.

Our products reach the market under our flagship brand IndiFrozen — and as private label for partners across the globe.

Manufacturing

State-of-the-art facility,
restaurant-grade craft.

Our plant in Ahmedabad is built to combine scale, precision and technology without losing the handwork that makes Indian food taste right. Dedicated lines for appetizers, gravies, breads and sweets run alongside blast freezers and IQF tunnels, with temperature-mapped cold storage and export documentation under one roof.

Handcrafted where it matters — shaping, stuffing, garnishing. Automated where it counts — dosing, freezing, metal detection, weighing and sealing. The result is a product that eats like a kitchen made it and ships like a factory built it.

IQF Freezing Tunnels

Each piece frozen individually within hours of cooking — loose, restaurant-quality pieces with no clumping.

Blast Freezing & Cold Storage

Rapid core-temperature pull-down to −18 °C and temperature-mapped storage until dispatch.

Hand-Finish Sections

Shaping, stuffing and garnish stages kept by hand for authentic form and texture.

Export-Ready Packing

Retail pouches, food-service cartons and custom private-label formats with compliant labelling.

Production Lines

Four lines, four disciplines.

Separate lines keep allergens, textures and process temperatures apart — and let us run a snack, a gravy, a bread and a sweet on the same day without compromise.

Line 01

Appetizers & Snacks

Forming, stuffing and hand-shaping stations feeding a continuous fryer and IQF tunnel. Samosas, tikkis, kebabs, spring rolls, nuggets and Gujarati farsan.

Steam-jacketed kettles for slow-cooked bases and curries, followed by hot-fill or portion-pack and blast freezing. Makhani, palak, chole, sambhar, shaak.

The Process

From kitchen to container.

  1. 01

    Sourcing & Inspection

    Vegetables, dairy, spices and packaging accepted only against specification, with supplier documentation on file.

  2. 02

    Prep & Batch Cooking

    Cleaning, cutting and slow batch cooking to a fixed process sheet — the same recipe, the same yield, every run.

  3. 03

    Forming & Hand Finish

    Shaping, stuffing and garnishing by hand where the format demands it, weight-checked piece by piece.

  4. 04

    IQF & Blast Freezing

    Individual quick freezing within hours of cooking, pulling the core down to −18 °C to lock texture and flavour.

  5. 05

    Metal Detection & Packing

    100% metal detection, checkweighing, then sealing into retail pouches, food-service cartons or private-label formats.

  6. 06

    Cold Storage & Dispatch

    Temperature-mapped storage, reefer loading and complete export documentation — plant to port without a break in the chain.
