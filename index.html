Our Story

A restaurant's soul,
a plant's discipline.

Most frozen food starts in a factory. Ours started at a restaurant table. Friends Kitchen — our catering and dining brand in Ahmedabad — spent years perfecting the biryanis, tikkis and gravies its guests kept coming back for.

FK Epicure Foods was built to take those exact recipes further. At our manufacturing plant on the Sarkhej–Sanand Highway, we cook them the same slow way, then freeze each piece individually within hours of cooking — no preservatives, no shortcuts — and ship them to commercial kitchens, QSRs and retail freezers in 35+ countries.

The rule hasn't changed since the first table: if a dish wouldn't earn a second helping at our own restaurant, it doesn't leave our plant.

The Plant

Built to freeze food the honest way.

Our facility on the Sarkhej–Sanand Highway, Ahmedabad, is where restaurant craft meets industrial discipline — handcrafted processes where they matter, precision freezing technology where it counts.

Dedicated lines for snacks, gravies, breads and sweets run alongside blast freezers and IQF tunnels, with temperature-mapped cold storage and export documentation handled under one roof.

IQF Freezing Lines

Each piece frozen individually within hours of cooking — loose, restaurant-quality pieces with no clumping, no sogginess.

Blast Freezing & Cold Storage

Rapid core-temperature pull-down to -18°C and temperature-mapped storage until dispatch.

Hand-Finish Sections

Shaping, stuffing and garnish stages kept by hand — because machines can't fold a samosa with love.

Export-Ready Packing

Retail pouches, food-service cartons and custom private-label formats with FDA-compliant labelling.

Farm to Freezer

How a dish becomes an export.

Chef-led small batches, cooked the slow restaurant way — gravies simmered for hours, breads rested properly, nothing rushed.

03

Freeze

Individually quick frozen within hours of cooking, pulled to -18°C — texture, nutrition and flavour locked in without preservatives.

04

Ship

Unbroken, temperature-monitored cold chain from our plant to ports and partner warehouses in 35+ countries — documentation included.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

We manufacture IQF frozen Indian foods at our plant in Ahmedabad — 100+ SKUs across appetizers & snacks, curries & gravies, Indian breads, rice & biryanis, sweets and sauces — supplied ready-to-cook and ready-to-eat to restaurants, QSRs, caterers and retailers worldwide.

IQF (Individually Quick Frozen) freezes each piece separately within hours of cooking, locking in texture, nutrition and flavour without preservatives. Pieces stay loose — your kitchen portions exactly what it needs, with zero waste.

Yes. We export to 35+ countries with complete documentation — FSSAI, ISO 22000, HACCP, Halal and Kosher certified, with FDA-compliant labelling. Write to info@fkepicurefoods.in with your market and volumes for pricing and MOQs.

Yes. We develop private label frozen Indian food ranges — exclusive recipes benchmarked to your market, custom piece counts and weights, and retail-ready packaging formats from pouches to microwavable trays.

Stored at -18°C, our IQF products typically hold 12–18 months' shelf life depending on the category, with batch-coded production dates and full traceability on every carton.

FK Epicure Foods grew out of Friends Kitchen, our restaurant and catering brand in Ahmedabad. Friends Kitchen and Café Affogato remain our sister brands — the live kitchens where every recipe is proven before it's scaled at the plant. IndiFrozen is our flagship frozen brand.
